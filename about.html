Our Journey & Shared Values

Founded in 2016 by a small group of computer science professors and creative designers, Amoo Academy set out with one direct objective: to replace boring theoretical manuals with practical, high-impact, real-world educational masterclasses.

We understand that modern students need more than just definitions. That is why our curricula align directly to dynamic hands-on files, downloadable e-books, and secure coding environments. Whether specializing in full-stack programming or customer support handling, we ensure you emerge job-ready.
